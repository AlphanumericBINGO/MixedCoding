Prabhu Vandana

AUM

Salutation to Lord Ganesha


Vakratunda Mahakaya,
Suryakoti Samaprabha
Nirvighnam Kuru Mey Deva,
Sarva Karyeshu Sarvadaa

The Lord with the curved trunk and mighty body, who has the magnificence of a Millon suns, I pray to you Oh lord, to remove the obstacles from all the actions I intend to perform


GAYATRI MANTRA


Om bhur bhuvasvah tatsa viturvaraneyum
Bhargo devasya Dhimahi dhiyo yonah prachodayat


O divine almighty God,
Our hearts are filled with darkness. Please make this darkness distant from us Please illuminate our intellect, which will lead us on the righteous path.


ASATOMAAM


Asatomaam sad gamaya
Tamasomaam jyotir gamaya
Mrityormaa amritum gamaya


lead us from untruth to truth, Lead us from darkness to light,
Kead us from death to immortality


GURUR BRAHMAA GURUR VISHNU


Gurur Brahma Gurur Vishnu Gurur Devo Maheshwarah
Guru Saakshaata Parabrahma Tasmai Shri Guruve Namah


Salutations to the noble Guru, who is Brahma, Vishnu and Maheshwarah, the direct Parabrahma, the supreme reality
